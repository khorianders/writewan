The Background

Furama Hotels International(FHI) is one of the largest global hotel chains situated in Singapore. FHI manages Furama Riverfront Hotel, which offers leisure and business travellers alike a well-appointed backdrop to indulge in restful play and invigorating work.

The Problem

The recent pandemic greatly affected our sales, we needed the best copy to work our marketing promotion to really engage the target market and let them know we are as excited to have them as much as they are eager to enjoy some place other than their homes. As one of the largest hotels chains in Asia, mediocre copy does not cut it for us.

The Solution

The copy: lean, clear and still highly compelling, with plently of white space for breathing room.

The core message: Furama is the place you need to go to relax and unwind after being stuck at home for months. We keep it affordable yet ensuring the quality of our service.
