Our Copywriting
Methodology

PROJECT DISCOVERY
& CUSTOMER PERSONA BUILDING

We begin with fully understanding your campaign goals, the solution you are marketing and the challenges you might be facing.

Following which, we will build out your customer persona (profile) to better understand their needs, desires and motivating factors to buy.

BRAND ANALYSIS
& RESEARCH

Our next step is to fully go through your brand assets and conduct research for the conception and narrative needed for the project.

We’ll create a strategy and action plan to maximize all the opportunities we see available for the implementation of your branding project.

BRAND COPYWRITING
& CONTENT CREATION

Our team of talented writers and specialists will then begin crafting your brand copy tailored to the purpose and nature of your advertising medium, both online and offline.

The end goal: Compelling brand copy that enhances your brand while communicate a strong value proposition to your readers.
