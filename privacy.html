Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority.

What information do we collect about you?

We collect information about you when you fill in a contact form, email us directly or subscribe to our mailing list. Website usage information is collected using cookies.
The information we collect about you may include; your name, email address, phone number, IP address company name, including any information you supply voluntarily.

Why we collect your data

We collect information about you to; fulfil your request e.g if you fill out the contact us form we use this information to contact you directly, understand your needs, create a project proposal and deliver the agreed service.
The data we collect from you will only be used depending on your needs, this may be a phone call, email correspondence, services and information we think may be of interest to you. In no way will we use the information shared to send unsolicited marketing emails.

Log Files

The Write Wan follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, The Write Wan uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.
For more general information on cookies, please read the "What Are Cookies" article on Cookie Consent website.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.
The Write Wan does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in The Write Wan. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.
